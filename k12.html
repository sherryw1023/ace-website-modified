U.S. Private Boarding & Day School Admissions

Personalized Application Process

We begin with a holistic evaluation of each student's academic background, English proficiency, personality traits, motivation for studying abroad, family context, and overall readiness. Through interviews with both students and parents, we provide honest, customized recommendations to determine if studying abroad is the right step.

What We Offer

  • School selection tailored to each student's goals
  • Application strategy and document preparation
  • Ongoing communication with schools, students, and families
  • Cultural preparation and transition planning

Transition Support

We offer full-service transition support, including:

  • Airport pickup and school arrival orientation
  • Bank and mobile plan setup
  • Emergency response and school conflict resolution
  • School transfers, if necessary

Our mission is to nurture well-rounded, globally minded individuals with strong academic skills, independence, resilience, leadership, and cross-cultural competence.

Guardian Support Services

As a trusted local guardian, ACE LLC maintains close contact with students, families, host families, and schools to ensure each student's well-being.

  • Build and maintain student progress profiles
  • Regular check-ins and progress reports to parents
  • Mental health and cultural adjustment support
  • Emergency response 24/7
  • Monthly cultural and social activities
  • Share insights about American culture and school expectations

Host Family Placement Services

Finding the right host family is key to a positive experience. We take this responsibility seriously.

  • Host families are referred by schools, friends, and community members
  • We conduct home visits, take photos/videos, and conduct family interviews
  • References are checked for all potential host families
  • We coordinate virtual interviews between the student's family and the host family
  • If both sides agree, a formal contract is signed
  • If not, we begin a new search immediately
  • Host Family Orientation: Workshops on cultural expectations, emergencies, and communication
  • Ongoing support to resolve issues and reassignments if needed
